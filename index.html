About Glyen

Our Story

Glyen was born from the realization that consistent, guided practice is the key to mastering programming. Too many developers get stuck in tutorial hell without making real progress.

We provide the structure, resources, and community support to help you build a consistent coding habit, develop a impressive GitHub portfolio, and accelerate your growth as a developer.
